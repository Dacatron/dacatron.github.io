Vehicle Compatibility


Currently, Dacatron products are designed to exclusively fit Toyota 4Runner (2025-Present) and Tacoma (2024-Present) equipped with JBL premium sound system. We are actively working on soon expanding compatibility to other trims levels.

Below is a list of current compatible trims:

    Toyota 4Runner (2025-Present):

    TRD Sport Premium
    TRD Off-Road Premium trims
    Limited trims
    Platinum
    Trailhunter
    TRD Pro

    Toyota Tacoma (2024-Present):

    TRD Sport trims equipped with Upgrade Package or Premium Package
    TRD Off-Road trims equipped with Upgrade Package or Premium Package
    Limited trims
    Trailhunter
    TRD Pro
